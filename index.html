Design Exploration

Studio Aura

One dance studio, fifteen radically different ways to present it. Each page explores a distinct visual direction — from ultra-minimal to maximalist, editorial to brutalist, neon noir to zen. Same studio, wildly different expressions.

Dance · Fitness · Movement

01

Minimal Luxe

High-end boutique studio feel. Warm off-whites, muted gold accents, serene whitespace, and whisper-thin typography.

minimalist luxury serene

02

Bold Urban

Street dance energy. Dark backgrounds, electric red and neon yellow accents, broken grids, and raw power.

urban bold street

03

Soft Organic

Earthy wellness studio aesthetic. Terracotta, sage green, and warm linen tones with rounded, inviting shapes.

earthy wellness warm

04

Dark Editorial

Moody magazine aesthetic. Near-black backgrounds, oversized italic serifs, and a Vogue-spread sensibility.

editorial moody magazine

05

Neon Noir

Late-night dance vibes. Deep midnight backgrounds, hot pink and cyan neon glows, and moody atmospherics.

neon dark nightlife

06

Retro Groovy

Disco funk energy. Burnt orange, mustard, and teal with rounded shapes, wavy edges, and 70s nostalgia.

retro groovy funky

07

Zen Minimal

Japanese-inspired contemplation. Extreme whitespace, delicate typography, vermillion accents, and quiet elegance.

zen minimal refined

08

Pastel Pop

Youthful and playful. Bubblegum pink, lilac, and mint with bouncy typography and energetic layouts.

playful pastel youthful

09

Brutalist

Raw, stark, anti-design. Monospace type, thick borders, broken grids, and deliberate visual tension.

brutalist raw anti-design

10

Warm Terracotta

Mediterranean sun. Burnt clay, olive, and sand tones with arch shapes, drop caps, and expressive warmth.

mediterranean warm expressive

11

Monochrome Ballet

High-contrast black and white. Bodoni typography, dramatic dance photography, and classical elegance.

monochrome classical dramatic

12

Glass Modern

Tech-forward glassmorphism. Deep navy with frosted glass cards, indigo-to-pink gradients, and app-like polish.

modern glass tech

13

Art Deco

1920s glamour meets jazz dance. Black and gold with geometric ornaments, sunburst patterns, and formal symmetry.

deco glamour geometric

14

Boho Flow

Free-spirited and organic. Sage, lavender, and terracotta with flowing shapes and a curated bohemian ease.

bohemian organic flowing

15

Enchanted Night

Starlit and magical. Deep navy with gold dust, twinkling accents, and a dreamlike, celestial atmosphere.

magical celestial dreamy
