Dance Forms & Fitness

Nine ways to move, breathe, and grow

Ballet

Classical technique and graceful expression for all levels.

Learn more
Our ballet classes focus on alignment, musicality, and the beauty of intentional movement. Beginners build a strong foundation while advanced students refine artistry through barre and center work. All levels welcome.
🌊

Contemporary

Fluid movement, floor work, and personal expression.

Learn more
Contemporary class explores the space between structure and freedom. Drawing from release technique, Limon, and improvisation, each session invites you to move authentically and expressively.
🔥

Hip-Hop

Groove, rhythm, and high-energy choreography.

Learn more
From foundational grooves to full choreography, our hip-hop classes bring energy and community to the studio. Expect current music, dynamic movement, and a lot of fun. No experience needed.
✨

Jazz

Dynamic, stylish movement rooted in rhythm and technique.

Learn more
Jazz class builds coordination, musicality, and performance quality. Classes blend classic jazz vocabulary with modern influences, keeping the energy high and the movement sharp.
💫

Salsa

Vibrant Latin rhythms and partner connection.

Learn more
Learn the fundamentals of salsa footwork, timing, and partner leading and following. Our classes emphasize connection, musicality, and the social joy of Latin dance. No partner required.
🌟

Bollywood

Expressive storytelling through vibrant Indian dance styles.

Learn more
Bollywood dance blends classical Indian movement with contemporary film choreography. Expect colorful expression, energetic footwork, and choreography that tells a story. Perfect for all fitness levels.
🎶

Zumba

Dance-fitness party with Latin and world music.

Learn more
Zumba is a full-body cardio celebration. Combining Latin rhythms with easy-to-follow choreography, each class feels less like a workout and more like a dance party. All fitness levels welcome.
🧘

Yoga

Mindful movement, breath work, and inner stillness.

Learn more
Our yoga classes blend vinyasa flow with restorative practices. Each session is designed to build strength, flexibility, and calm. We emphasize breath-body connection and offer modifications for every level.
🤸

Gymnastics

Strength, flexibility, and body awareness training.

Learn more
Our gymnastics program focuses on foundational skills, tumbling, and body conditioning. Designed for adults of all levels, classes build strength, balance, and spatial awareness in a supportive environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit

Do I need dance experience to join a class?
Not at all. Many of our classes are designed specifically for beginners, and our instructors offer modifications for all levels. Whether you have danced your whole life or have never taken a class, you are welcome here.
What should I wear to class?
Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to move freely. For ballet, fitted clothing and ballet slippers are recommended. For yoga, stretchy fabrics work best. Hip-hop and jazz classes are great in sneakers and athletic wear. We will share specific recommendations when you register.
How do I register for classes?
You can register by emailing us at hello@studioaura.com or calling (555) 234-8901. Walk-ins are welcome when space permits, but we recommend reserving your spot in advance to guarantee a place.
What is your cancellation policy?
We ask for at least 12 hours notice for class cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may forfeit the class credit. Workshop cancellations require 48 hours notice for a full refund. We understand life happens, so please reach out if you have special circumstances.
Do you offer private lessons?
Yes, we offer private and semi-private lessons in all dance forms and yoga. Private sessions are a wonderful way to work on specific goals, prepare for events, or simply enjoy personalized attention. Contact us for pricing and availability.
Is there parking available?
There is a free parking lot directly behind our building on Movement Lane, as well as metered street parking throughout the Arts District. The studio is also accessible by public transit, with the Arts District station a five-minute walk away.
