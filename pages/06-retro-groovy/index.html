Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got groovy answers.

1 Do I need dance experience to join?
Not at all! Many of our classes are designed for absolute beginners. We label every class by level -- Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced -- so you can find the perfect fit. Our instructors are patient, encouraging, and love helping newcomers find their groove.
2 What should I wear to class?
Comfortable, stretchy clothing that lets you move freely. For ballet, leotards and ballet shoes are recommended. For hip-hop, sneakers with good support work best. Yoga classes are barefoot. When in doubt, just wear something you can dance in!
3 How do I register for classes?
You can sign up by calling us, emailing, or visiting the studio in person. Walk-ins are welcome for most classes, but we recommend reserving your spot for popular sessions and workshops as they fill up quickly!
4 What is your cancellation policy?
We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for class cancellations. Workshop cancellations require 48 hours' notice for a full refund. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee. We understand life happens -- just give us a heads up when you can!
5 Do you offer private lessons?
Yes! Private lessons are available in all dance styles. Whether you're preparing for a wedding dance, audition, or just want one-on-one attention, our instructors are happy to work with you. Contact us to schedule and discuss rates.
6 Is there parking available?
There's a free parking lot directly behind the studio with 30 spaces. Street parking is also available along Movement Lane. For evening classes, the Arts District garage one block east offers affordable hourly rates. We're also easily accessible by bus and bike!
