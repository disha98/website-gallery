What We Teach

Dance Forms & Fitness

01

Ballet

Classical
Learn More

Foundational technique meets artistic expression. Our ballet program builds strength, flexibility, and grace — from beginner barre work to advanced pointe. Classes focus on alignment, musicality, and the sheer beauty of controlled movement.

02

Contemporary

Expressive
Learn More

Fluid, emotional, boundary-breaking. Contemporary at Studio Aura blends release technique, floor work, and improvisation. Expect to explore weight, breath, and momentum in ways that challenge your body and your assumptions.

03

Hip-Hop

Street Energy
Learn More

The heartbeat of Studio Aura. Popping, locking, breaking, krumping, and choreography that hits hard. Our hip-hop program honors the culture while pushing the art forward. Bring your attitude. Leave your ego at the door.

04

Jazz

Dynamic
Learn More

Sharp isolations, explosive turns, and rhythm that doesn't quit. Jazz at Studio Aura covers classic Broadway to modern jazz funk. Perfect for dancers who love performance energy and technical precision in equal measure.

05

Salsa

Partner / Solo
Learn More

Feel the rhythm, find the connection. Our salsa classes cover Cuban, LA-style, and on2 timing. Solo shines and partner work both welcome. No partner required — we rotate so everyone dances with everyone.

06

Bollywood

High Energy
Learn More

Color, drama, and unstoppable energy. Bollywood dance fuses classical Indian forms with modern choreography and cinematic flair. Every class is a celebration — expressive hands, dynamic footwork, and full-body storytelling.

07

Zumba

Fitness / Cardio
Learn More

Dance your way to fitness. Zumba at Studio Aura is a high-energy cardio party blending Latin rhythms, Afrobeats, and pop. No experience needed — just show up, follow the vibe, and sweat. Every class burns 400–800 calories.

08

Yoga

Mind & Body
Learn More

Strength, flexibility, stillness. Our yoga program complements every dance form with vinyasa flows, deep stretches, and breathwork. Designed for dancers and non-dancers alike — the perfect counterbalance to high-intensity training.

09

Gymnastics

Power / Agility
Learn More

Flips, tumbling, aerial awareness. Our gymnastics program builds explosive power and spatial confidence. From basic rolls to advanced aerials — this is where dancers come to defy gravity and add acrobatic vocabulary to their movement.

Got Questions?

FAQs

01 Do I need prior dance experience?

Not at all. Every class at Studio Aura is designed with multiple levels in mind. Our instructors break down movements for beginners while offering advanced variations for experienced dancers. Just show up ready to move.

02 What should I wear to class?

Comfortable clothing you can move freely in. For ballet, fitted attire and ballet shoes are recommended. For hip-hop and Zumba, sneakers work great. Yoga classes are barefoot. We have changing rooms and lockers on-site.

03 How do I register for classes?

Walk in, call us at (555) 234-8901, or email hello@studioaura.com. We offer drop-in classes, monthly memberships, and class packages. First class is always free — no commitment, no pressure.

04 What is your cancellation policy?

Cancel up to 4 hours before class for a full credit. Late cancellations and no-shows are charged at the drop-in rate. Monthly members get two free late cancellations per month. We keep it fair and flexible.

05 Do you offer private lessons?

Yes. Private and semi-private sessions are available in all nine disciplines. Great for wedding choreography, performance prep, or accelerated learning. Contact us to schedule and discuss rates.

06 Is there parking available?

Street parking is available on Movement Lane and surrounding blocks. There's also a public parking garage on 3rd & Arts Blvd, a 2-minute walk from the studio. We're also accessible by bus routes 14 and 22.
