About Us

More Than a Studio

Studio Aura is a premium dance and fitness destination where artistry meets athleticism. Our state-of-the-art facility features sprung hardwood floors, floor-to-ceiling mirrors, and a sound system that makes every beat come alive.

Whether you are a seasoned performer or stepping onto the floor for the first time, our world-class instructors create an inclusive, empowering environment where every body finds its rhythm.

What We Offer

Dance Forms & Fitness

From classical elegance to high-energy grooves, find the movement style that speaks to you.

🩰

Ballet

Classical

Timeless grace and precision. Build strength, flexibility, and poise through classical technique.

More details
Our ballet program covers barre work, centre practice, adagio, and allegro. Classes range from beginner foundations to advanced pointe work. All levels focus on proper alignment, musicality, and expressive movement. Ages 8 and up welcome.
🌊

Contemporary

Expressive

Fluid, emotive movement that blends ballet foundations with modern freedom of expression.

More details
Explore floorwork, improvisation, release technique, and partnering. Our contemporary classes encourage creative exploration while building technical vocabulary. Influenced by Limon, Graham, and Forsythe methodologies.
🔥

Hip-Hop

High Energy

Street-style grooves, isolations, and choreography set to the hottest tracks.

More details
Learn popping, locking, breaking foundations, and commercial choreography. Classes cover rhythm training, freestyle development, and performance-ready routines. No experience necessary for beginner sessions.
✨

Jazz

Dynamic

High-energy technique with sharp movements, turns, and leaps that command the stage.

More details
From Broadway-style to contemporary jazz funk, our classes build coordination, stamina, and showmanship. Emphasis on clean lines, dynamic performance quality, and versatile movement vocabulary.
💫

Salsa

Partner Dance

Infectious Latin rhythms, footwork patterns, and partner connection that lights up the room.

More details
Learn salsa on1 and on2 timing, cross-body leads, spins, and social dance etiquette. Solo and partner classes available. Monthly social dance nights included with membership. No partner required to join.
🌟

Bollywood

Vibrant

Expressive, colorful choreography blending classical Indian forms with cinematic flair.

More details
A fusion of Kathak, Bhangra, folk, and modern Bollywood film choreography. Classes focus on facial expression (abhinaya), hand gestures (mudras), and high-energy group formations. Perfect for wedding choreography prep.
🎶

Zumba

Cardio

Dance-based cardio that feels like a party. Burn calories while having the time of your life.

More details
Licensed Zumba instruction combining merengue, cumbia, reggaeton, and pop music. Each session burns 400-800 calories. No dance experience needed, just bring energy and water. Modifications available for all fitness levels.
🧘

Yoga

Mind & Body

Strengthen, stretch, and center yourself with flows designed for dancers and movers.

More details
Vinyasa, Hatha, and restorative yoga classes tailored for the dance community. Focus on injury prevention, flexibility gains, breath work, and mental clarity. Complements all dance forms and enhances overall performance.
🤸

Gymnastics

Acrobatic

Tumbling, acrobatics, and aerial foundations to add gravity-defying elements to your movement.

More details
Progressive skill-building from basic rolls and cartwheels to aerials and back handsprings. Safety-first approach with padded training areas. Builds core strength, spatial awareness, and confidence. Cross-trains perfectly with dance.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before your first class.

Do I need prior dance experience?
Not at all! We offer beginner-friendly classes in every dance form. Our instructors are skilled at breaking down movements for newcomers while keeping experienced dancers challenged. Just bring comfortable clothes and an open mind.
What should I wear to class?
Wear comfortable, form-fitting clothing that allows full range of motion. For ballet, leotards and ballet shoes are recommended. For hip-hop and jazz, sneakers work great. Yoga classes are best in barefoot-friendly athletic wear. We have a small retail section if you need anything.
How do memberships work?
We offer flexible plans: drop-in classes ($25 each), class packs (10 for $200, 20 for $360), and unlimited monthly memberships ($149/month). Annual members get a 20% discount. All memberships include access to open gym hours and monthly social events.
Can I try a class before committing?
Absolutely! Your first class is free with no obligation. We also offer a two-week introductory pass for $39 that gives you unlimited access to try as many different classes as you like. It is the best way to find your favorites.
Is there parking available?
Yes, we have a dedicated parking lot behind the building with 40 spaces, free for all students during class hours. There is also street parking along Movement Lane and a public garage one block east. We are accessible by the Blue Line metro, Arts District stop.
Do you offer private lessons?
Yes! Private and semi-private lessons are available with all our instructors. Perfect for wedding choreography, audition prep, or accelerated learning. Rates start at $75 per hour. Contact us to schedule a session with your preferred instructor.
What is your cancellation policy?
Classes can be cancelled up to 4 hours before start time with no penalty. Late cancellations and no-shows will forfeit the class credit. For workshops, full refunds are available up to 7 days before the event; 50% refund within 3-7 days.
