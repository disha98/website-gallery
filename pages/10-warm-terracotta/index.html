Welcome

Where Movement Meets Soul

Nestled in the heart of the Arts District, Studio Aura is a sun-drenched haven for dancers, movers, and seekers of all levels. Our terracotta-warmed floors have held the footsteps of beginners finding their rhythm and seasoned performers refining their craft. We believe that dance is not a performance — it is a conversation between your body and the world. Here, every class is an invitation to discover the language your body already knows.

Our instructors bring decades of combined experience across classical, contemporary, and global dance traditions. Whether you arrive with years of training or simply a curiosity to move, you will find a class that meets you exactly where you are.

Classes

Dance Forms & Fitness

From the precision of ballet to the fire of salsa, from centering yoga to high-energy Zumba — find the practice that speaks to you.

Classical

Ballet

Grace, strength, and discipline in every movement. Build a foundation of technique and artistry.

Learn more

Our ballet classes follow the Vaganova method, emphasizing clean lines, turnout, and musicality. Beginner classes focus on barre fundamentals and basic center work, while intermediate and advanced levels incorporate adagio, allegro, and pointe preparation. Classes are accompanied by live piano on Tuesdays and Saturdays.

Expressive

Contemporary

Fluid, grounded, and deeply expressive. Explore the intersection of technique and personal voice.

Learn more

Drawing from Limon, Graham, and release techniques, our contemporary classes invite you to explore weight, momentum, and floor work. Each session includes guided improvisation to help you develop your own movement vocabulary. Perfect for dancers looking to expand beyond classical forms.

Street

Hip-Hop

High energy, rhythm-driven, and powerful. Learn choreography rooted in street dance culture.

Learn more

From foundational grooves and isolations to full choreography, our hip-hop classes cover a range of styles including popping, locking, krumping, and commercial choreography. All levels welcome — we emphasize feeling the music and building confidence on the floor.

Classic

Jazz

Sharp, dynamic, and theatrical. Precision footwork meets expressive storytelling.

Learn more

Rooted in the tradition of Fosse and Luigi, our jazz classes focus on isolations, syncopation, and performance quality. You will build flexibility, coordination, and stage presence through warm-ups, across-the-floor combinations, and choreography set to classic and contemporary music.

Latin

Salsa

Infectious rhythm, partner connection, and pure joy. Dance the heartbeat of Latin culture.

Learn more

Our salsa classes cover On1 and On2 timing, basic turn patterns, partner lead-and-follow technique, and body movement (Cuban motion). No partner required — we rotate throughout class. Monthly social dance nights give you the chance to practice in a lively, supportive atmosphere.

Global

Bollywood

Vibrant, celebratory, and full of life. Learn choreography inspired by Indian cinema and folk traditions.

Learn more

Our Bollywood classes blend classical Indian dance elements (Kathak, Bharatanatyam) with modern film choreography. Expect high-energy routines, expressive hand gestures (mudras), and joyful group formations. Great for all fitness levels — the focus is on expression and celebration.

Fitness

Zumba

A high-energy dance fitness party. Latin rhythms, easy-to-follow moves, and a full-body workout.

Learn more

Zumba at Studio Aura blends merengue, cumbia, reggaeton, and samba-inspired movements into a heart-pumping cardio workout. No dance experience necessary — just bring your energy. Each 50-minute class burns 400–600 calories while keeping you smiling the entire time.

Wellness

Yoga

Breathe, stretch, and center yourself. Build flexibility and mindfulness to complement your dance practice.

Learn more

Our vinyasa-based yoga classes are designed with dancers in mind, focusing on hip openers, back flexibility, core stability, and breath control. Morning sessions are gentle and restorative; select evening sessions offer a more dynamic flow. Props provided. All levels welcome.

Acrobatic

Gymnastics

Strength, agility, and fearless movement. Build acrobatic skills in a supportive environment.

Learn more

Our gymnastics program covers floor tumbling, handstands, cartwheels, aerials, and basic acrobatic partnering. Safety is paramount — all sessions use crash mats and spotting techniques. A great complement to contemporary dance and an exciting way to build strength and spatial awareness.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior dance experience to join?

Not at all. Many of our classes are designed for absolute beginners. Our instructors create a welcoming, judgment-free environment where you can explore movement at your own pace. Check the level dots on each class card to find the right fit for you.

What should I wear to class?

Comfortable clothing that allows you to move freely. For ballet, form-fitting attire is recommended so instructors can see your alignment. For hip-hop and Zumba, sneakers work well. Yoga and contemporary classes are typically done barefoot. We have changing rooms and lockers available.

How do I sign up for a class?

You can drop in to any open class, but we recommend reserving your spot in advance by calling us at (555) 234-8901 or emailing hello@studioaura.com. Workshop registration requires advance booking as spaces are limited.

Do you offer membership packages?

Yes. We offer single class drop-ins ($20), 10-class packs ($160), and unlimited monthly memberships ($120/month). Student and senior discounts are available. Ask at the front desk or contact us for details.

Is there parking available?

There is a public parking garage one block east of the studio on 3rd Street, and metered street parking is available along Movement Lane. We also have a bike rack at our entrance. The Arts District Metro stop is a 5-minute walk away.

Can I book the studio for private events?

Absolutely. Our main studio space is available for private rehearsals, small performances, photo shoots, and private group classes. Contact us at hello@studioaura.com for availability and rates.

What is your cancellation policy?

For regular classes, we ask for at least 4 hours notice if you need to cancel your reservation. For workshops, cancellations made 48 hours or more in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours may be applied as credit toward future workshops.
