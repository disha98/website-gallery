What We Teach

Dance Forms & Fitness

Nine disciplines, one philosophy: move with intention, express with authenticity.

Ballet +
Classical technique meets contemporary sensibility. Our ballet classes build strength, grace, and precision through barre work, center floor combinations, and across-the-floor progressions. Suitable for all levels, from absolute beginners to advanced dancers.
Contemporary +
Fluid, expressive, and deeply personal. Contemporary classes explore weight, breath, and space through improvisation and choreography. Drawing from Limon, Graham, and release technique, these classes invite you to find your own movement language.
Hip-Hop +
Raw energy channeled through rhythm and groove. Our hip-hop classes cover foundational styles, choreography, and freestyle. Expect hard-hitting combinations set to the latest tracks and classic beats that make you move.
Jazz +
Dynamic, rhythmic, and theatrical. Jazz classes blend sharp isolations with smooth transitions, developing your musicality and stage presence. From Broadway to street jazz, there is a style for every dancer here.
Salsa +
Feel the pulse of Latin rhythm in every step. Our salsa classes teach timing, partner connection, and styling through progressive combinations. Whether you are preparing for social dancing or performance, we will get you moving with confidence.
Bollywood +
Vibrant, joyful, and cinematic. Bollywood dance fuses classical Indian forms with modern choreography set to the biggest hits from Indian cinema. A high-energy, feel-good class that celebrates color, culture, and pure dance joy.
Zumba +
A dance-fitness party that does not feel like a workout. Zumba classes blend Latin and international music with accessible, fun choreography. High energy, low barrier to entry. Come as you are and leave drenched in endorphins.
Yoga +
The perfect complement to dance. Our yoga classes range from gentle restorative flows to dynamic vinyasa sequences, all designed to build flexibility, core strength, and the mindful body awareness that elevates every other discipline.
Gymnastics +
Build acrobatic confidence through foundational tumbling, balance, and aerial awareness. Our gymnastics sessions focus on strength, spatial orientation, and controlled power. Ideal for dancers looking to expand their physical vocabulary.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need prior dance experience? +
Not at all. Many of our classes are designed for absolute beginners. We offer tiered levels across most disciplines, and our instructors are skilled at making everyone feel welcome and capable from the very first class.
What should I wear? +
Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to move freely. For ballet, we recommend fitted attire and ballet slippers. For yoga, stretchy fabrics work best. For hip-hop and Zumba, athletic wear and clean sneakers are ideal.
How do I register for a class? +
You can register online through our booking system, call us at (555) 234-8901, or simply drop in. We recommend reserving your spot for popular evening classes and workshops, as they tend to fill up quickly.
What is your cancellation policy? +
We ask for at least 12 hours notice for class cancellations. Late cancellations and no-shows will forfeit the class credit. Workshop cancellations made more than 48 hours in advance are fully refundable.
Do you offer private lessons? +
Yes. Private and semi-private sessions are available in all disciplines. These are perfect for wedding choreography, performance preparation, or accelerated learning. Contact us to discuss scheduling and rates.
Is there parking available? +
We have a small lot behind the building with complimentary parking for members. Street parking is also available along Movement Lane and adjacent blocks. The Arts District Metro stop is a five-minute walk away.
