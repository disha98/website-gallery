About the Studio

Who We Are

Studio Aura is a raw space for raw movement. We reject polish. We reject perfection. We believe dance is a language spoken by the body before the mind catches up. Founded in 2018 in the heart of the Arts District, we have built a community of movers, shakers, breakers, and makers.

No mirrors. No judgement. Just the floor, the music, and your body doing exactly what it needs to do.

Location

Three floors. 12,000 sq ft of sprung hardwood. Industrial ceilings. Exposed ductwork. The kind of space that makes you want to move the second you walk in.

Dance Forms & Fitness

01
Ballet

Classical technique stripped to its core. Barre work, center combinations, port de bras. We train precision and control, but never at the expense of artistry. Pointe classes available for intermediate and advanced students.

CLASSICAL
02
Contemporary

Floorwork, release technique, improvisation, contact. The intersection of everything: ballet foundations, modern principles, the freedom to collapse and rebuild. Our signature program.

MODERN
03
Hip-Hop

Breaking, locking, popping, krumping, freestyle. Street culture and rhythm-driven movement. High energy, high impact, high expression. All levels from foundation grooves to advanced choreography.

STREET

Jazz

Sharp isolations, syncopation, attitude. From classic Broadway jazz to commercial contemporary. Focus on musicality, performance quality, and technical execution.

THEATRICAL

Salsa

Cuban-style salsa, LA-style on-1, New York mambo on-2. Partner work and solo body movement (shines). Social dance nights every Friday. No partner needed.

LATIN
06
Bollywood

High-energy choreography fusing classical Indian forms with modern Bollywood film style. Expressive, celebratory, and wildly fun. Great cardio workout disguised as pure joy.

FILM

Zumba

Dance fitness party. Latin-inspired cardio that burns 500-800 calories per session. No dance experience needed. Just show up and move. All ages, all bodies.

FITNESS
08
Yoga

Vinyasa flow, power yoga, and restorative sessions. The essential counterbalance to intense dance training. Breath, alignment, flexibility, mental clarity.

WELLNESS

Gymnastics

Tumbling, acrobatics, aerial conditioning. Build the strength and control that elevates your dance. Open floor work, conditioning circuits, and progressive skill development.

FAQs

01

Do I need dance experience to join?

No. Every class has a beginner-friendly option. We mark levels clearly in our schedule: (B) beginner, (I) intermediate, (A) advanced. Most classes are open-level, meaning the instructor adapts to whoever shows up.

02

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothing you can move freely in. Bare feet or dance shoes depending on the class. Ballet requires proper ballet slippers. Hip-hop requires clean sneakers. We sell basics at the front desk.

03

How do drop-in classes work?

Walk in 10 minutes before class, sign the waiver (first time only), pay at the desk or show your class pass. $22 per drop-in. 10-class pass: $180. Unlimited monthly: $150.

04

Do you offer private lessons?

Yes. Private lessons are available in all dance forms. $80/hour for individual, $120/hour for pairs. Book via email or at the front desk. 48-hour cancellation policy applies.

05

Is parking available?

Street parking on Movement Lane and 5th Ave. Public lot two blocks east ($5 flat after 5PM). Bike racks out front. We are one block from the Arts District Metro station.

06

Can I rent the studio space?

Yes. Studio A (main floor, 4,000 sq ft) and Studio B (second floor, 2,500 sq ft) are available for rehearsals, events, and photo/video shoots. Email us for rates and availability.

07

Do you have classes for kids?

Ages 5-12: Saturday mornings (ballet, hip-hop, gymnastics). Ages 13-17: after-school programs Monday-Thursday. Summer intensive camps in June and July. Contact us for the youth schedule.
