About the Studio

A Space for
Radical Movement

Studio Aura was born from a simple conviction: that the body in motion is the most honest form of storytelling. Founded in 2019 in the heart of the Arts District, our studio has become a gathering place for those who believe dance is not decoration—it is language.

We house nine distinct disciplines under one roof, from the precision of classical ballet to the raw energy of hip-hop, from the meditative flow of yoga to the explosive joy of Bollywood. Every class is led by working artists and performers who bring the stage into the studio.

Our 4,000-square-foot space features sprung hardwood floors, floor-to-ceiling mirrors, a professional sound system, and natural light that pours through arched warehouse windows. This is not a gym. This is a theatre of the everyday.

02 — Disciplines

Dance Forms
& Fitness

“To watch us dance is to hear our hearts speak.”
— Hopi Indian Saying

  • Ballet Classical & Neoclassical

    From barre fundamentals to centre work and petit allegro, our ballet program honors the classical tradition while embracing contemporary interpretation. Open to all levels. Pointe classes available by instructor invitation.

  • Contemporary Floor work & Improvisation

    Rooted in release technique and contact improvisation, our contemporary classes explore weight, momentum, and breath. Expect floor work, partnering, and guided improvisational scores that push the boundaries of personal expression.

  • Hip-Hop Street Styles & Choreography

    From foundational grooves and isolation drills to full choreography sets, our hip-hop program covers breaking, popping, locking, and freestyle. High energy, community-driven, and always rooted in the culture.

  • Jazz Broadway & Commercial

    Sharp isolations, dynamic turns, and theatrical flair. Our jazz classes draw from both Broadway and commercial traditions, building strength, flexibility, and stage presence in equal measure.

  • Salsa Partner & Solo Styling

    Learn the rhythmic intricacies of salsa with classes covering on1 and on2 timing, cross-body leads, turn patterns, and solo shines. No partner required—we rotate throughout class.

  • Bollywood Filmi & Folk Fusion

    A vibrant fusion of classical Indian dance, folk traditions, and contemporary Bollywood choreography. High-energy routines set to the latest hits and timeless classics. All levels welcome—expressiveness matters more than experience.

  • Zumba Latin-Inspired Cardio

    A high-energy fitness party blending Latin and world rhythms with easy-to-follow choreography. No dance experience needed. Just show up, move, and leave drenched in endorphins.

  • Yoga Vinyasa & Restorative

    Complement your dance practice with vinyasa flow for strength and flexibility, or restorative sessions for deep recovery. Our yoga classes are designed specifically with dancers and movers in mind.

  • Gymnastics Tumbling & Acrobatics

    Build aerial awareness, tumbling skills, and acrobatic strength. From cartwheels and handstands to back tucks and aerials, our gymnastics program develops the fearless physicality that elevates every other discipline.

05 — Questions

Frequently
Asked

Do I need prior dance experience to take a class?
Not at all. Most of our classes offer beginner-friendly options, and our instructors are skilled at meeting students where they are. We recommend starting with an introductory or all-levels class.
What should I wear to class?
Comfortable, form-fitting clothing that allows full range of motion. Ballet requires ballet slippers; hip-hop requires clean sneakers. Yoga and contemporary are typically done barefoot. We sell basic supplies at the front desk.
How do drop-in classes work?
Simply arrive 10 minutes before class, check in at the front desk, and you are in. Drop-in rate is $22 per class. We also offer 5-class and 10-class packs, plus unlimited monthly memberships.
Can I try multiple disciplines?
Absolutely—we encourage it. Our monthly unlimited pass gives you access to every class on the schedule. Many of our students cross-train across three or four disciplines.
Do you offer private lessons?
Yes. Private and semi-private sessions are available in all nine disciplines. These can be booked directly with individual instructors or through the front desk. Ideal for wedding choreography, audition prep, or personal goals.
Is the studio available for private events?
Our main studio space is available for private rental on Sunday evenings and by special arrangement. Perfect for rehearsals, photo shoots, small performances, and private workshops. Contact us for rates.
