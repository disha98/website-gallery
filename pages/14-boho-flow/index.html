Our Story

Studio Aura was born from a simple belief: movement is medicine. Nestled in the heart of the Arts District, our sun-drenched studio is a sanctuary for anyone who has ever felt the pull of music in their bones.

Whether you are a seasoned dancer or stepping onto the floor for the first time, you belong here. We offer a rich tapestry of dance forms and fitness classes, each led by passionate instructors who nurture your unique journey.

Our space is designed to feel like a warm exhale. Natural light pours through arched windows, plants trail from reclaimed wood shelves, and the air smells faintly of sage and possibility.

Dance Forms & Fitness

Explore our offerings — there is something for every body and every spirit.

Classical

Ballet

Graceful, disciplined, and timeless. Our ballet classes range from gentle beginner barre work to advanced pointe technique. Build strength, poise, and an intimate connection to music through classical movement.

Expressive

Contemporary

Learn more

Fluid, emotive, and boundary-breaking. Contemporary dance blends technique with personal expression. Explore floor work, improvisation, and partnering in a class that encourages you to move authentically.

Urban

Hip-Hop

Learn more

High energy, bold, and rooted in culture. From foundational grooves to intricate choreography, our hip-hop classes celebrate self-expression, rhythm, and the joy of moving with community.

Classic

Jazz

Learn more

Dynamic, theatrical, and endlessly fun. Jazz dance combines sharp technique with show-stopping style. Expect turns, leaps, isolations, and choreography that makes you feel alive.

Latin

Salsa

Learn more

Fiery, social, and irresistibly rhythmic. Our salsa classes teach footwork, partner connection, and musicality. No partner needed — just bring your willingness to move and smile.

World

Bollywood

Learn more

Colorful, joyful, and cinematic. Bollywood dance draws from Indian classical and folk traditions, set to the vibrant soundtracks of Indian cinema. A full-body celebration of culture and movement.

Fitness

Zumba

Learn more

A dance-fitness party set to Latin and global beats. Zumba is for everyone — no dance experience needed. Burn calories, boost your mood, and leave drenched in joy (and maybe a little sweat).

Mind & Body

Yoga

Learn more

Grounding, restorative, and deeply centering. Our yoga offerings include Vinyasa flow, gentle Hatha, and restorative Yin. The perfect complement to dance — or a beautiful practice on its own.

Athletic

Gymnastics

Learn more

Strength, flexibility, and aerial awareness. Our gymnastics classes cover tumbling, balance, and acrobatic fundamentals. For those who want to defy gravity and build fearless body control.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

Do I need dance experience to join?

Not at all. Many of our classes are designed for absolute beginners. Every instructor creates a welcoming, judgment-free environment. You just need to show up — we will take care of the rest.

What should I wear to class?

Comfortable clothing that allows you to move freely. For ballet, fitted attire and ballet slippers are recommended. For hip-hop and jazz, supportive sneakers work best. Yoga classes are typically barefoot. We will share specific recommendations when you register.

How do I register for a class?

You can register by calling us at (555) 234-8901, emailing hello@studioaura.com, or visiting us in person at the studio. Drop-ins are welcome for most classes, but we recommend registering in advance for workshops and specialty sessions.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand life happens. Classes can be cancelled up to 4 hours before the scheduled start time with no penalty. Late cancellations and no-shows will be charged the full class fee. Workshop cancellations require 48 hours notice for a full refund.

Do you offer private lessons?

Yes, we offer private and semi-private lessons in all dance forms. Private sessions are a wonderful way to accelerate your learning, prepare for a special event, or simply enjoy personalized attention. Contact us to schedule.

Is there parking available?

Yes, there is a free parking lot directly behind the studio with space for 20 vehicles. Street parking is also available along Movement Lane. For those who prefer public transit, the Arts District station is a 5-minute walk away.
