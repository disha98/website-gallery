About Us

A space where joy meets movement and every body belongs.

📍 247 Movement Lane, Arts District, Downtown

Our vibrant community of dancers, movers, and shakers comes together every day to learn, laugh, and grow. With world-class instructors and a schedule packed with everything from ballet to Bollywood, there's always something new to try!

Dance Forms & Fitness

From graceful ballet to fiery Bollywood beats — find your rhythm.

Ballet

All Levels

Classical technique meets graceful expression. Build strength, poise, and elegance.

More info

Our ballet program covers barre work, center combinations, across-the-floor progressions, and petite/grande allegro. Classes range from absolute beginner to advanced pointe work. Ages 5 and up welcome.

Contemporary

Intermediate+

Fluid, emotional movement that blends ballet with modern technique.

More info

Explore floorwork, release technique, improvisation, and partnering. Our contemporary classes draw from Limon, Graham, and Forsythe influences. Some ballet experience recommended.

Hip-Hop

Beginner Friendly

High-energy grooves, isolations, and choreography to the latest hits.

More info

Learn popping, locking, breaking fundamentals, and choreography-based routines. Our hip-hop classes are super welcoming — no experience needed. Great cardio workout too!

Jazz

All Levels

Sharp, sassy, and fun. Broadway-style jazz with a modern twist.

More info

From classic jazz fundamentals to musical theatre choreography and commercial jazz styles. Build technique through isolations, kicks, turns, and leaps. Perfect for performers!

Salsa

Beginner Friendly

Spicy Latin rhythms, partner work, and social dance skills.

More info

Learn salsa basics, cross-body leads, turn patterns, and styling. Solo and partner options available. We host monthly socials so you can practice your moves!

Bollywood

Beginner Friendly

Vibrant, expressive dance to Bollywood's biggest and brightest tracks.

More info

A fusion of classical Indian dance, folk styles, and modern choreography set to Bollywood music. Expect lots of expression, hand gestures, and pure joy. No experience needed!

Zumba

Beginner Friendly

Dance-fitness party! Burn calories while having the time of your life.

More info

High-energy cardio dance set to Latin, pop, and world music beats. No dance experience necessary — just follow along and have fun. Burns 400-800 calories per session!

Yoga

All Levels

Stretch, breathe, and find your center. The perfect complement to dance.

More info

Vinyasa and Hatha styles offered. Focus on flexibility, strength, balance, and mindfulness. Great for injury prevention and recovery. Special yoga-for-dancers sessions available weekly.

Gymnastics

Intermediate+

Tumbling, flexibility, and acrobatic elements for dancers and movers.

More info

Build acrobatic skills including cartwheels, walkovers, aerials, and tumbling passes. Designed for dancers who want to add tricks to their repertoire. Proper spotting and progressions ensured.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got sparkly answers!

Do I need any dance experience to join?

Nope, not at all! Many of our classes are beginner-friendly and our instructors love working with first-timers. Just bring comfy clothes and an open mind. We'll handle the rest!

What should I wear to class?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to move freely. For ballet, we recommend fitted attire and ballet slippers. For hip-hop and Zumba, sneakers work great. Yoga classes are best in stretchy, breathable fabrics. We have a small retail section if you need anything!

How do I sign up for a class?

You can drop in for any regular class, but we recommend reserving your spot in advance by calling us at (555) 234-8901 or emailing hello@studioaura.com. Workshops require pre-registration as spots are limited.

What are your pricing options?

We offer drop-in rates ($20/class), class packs (5 for $85, 10 for $150), and unlimited monthly memberships ($120/month). Students and seniors get 15% off. Family bundles are also available!

Do you offer kids' classes?

Absolutely! We have dedicated classes for kids aged 5-8 and 8-12, primarily on Saturday mornings. We offer ballet, hip-hop, and creative movement for young dancers. Summer camps are available too!

Is there parking available?

Yes! We have a free parking lot behind the studio with 30 spaces. Street parking is also available on Movement Lane. The Arts District metro stop is a 5-minute walk away for those using public transit.
