Our Story

Studio Aura was born from a dream: a place where movement becomes magic, where the rhythm of the body meets the rhythm of the cosmos. Tucked in the heart of the Arts District, our studio is a sanctuary for dancers, movers, and dreamers of every kind.

Step through our doors and leave the ordinary behind. Here, beneath vaulted ceilings and soft amber light, you will find classes that challenge, inspire, and transform. Whether you are taking your first tentative steps or perfecting a lifelong craft, Studio Aura holds space for you.

We believe that dance is not performance alone; it is communion with something deeper, something luminous. Come as you are. Leave a little more radiant.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need prior dance experience to join?

Not at all. Many of our classes welcome absolute beginners. Each session is designed so that newcomers feel supported while more experienced dancers are still challenged. Just bring comfortable clothing and an open heart.

What should I wear to class?

Wear comfortable, form-fitting clothing that allows free movement. For ballet, leotards and ballet slippers are recommended. For hip-hop and Zumba, sneakers with good support work best. Yoga classes are typically barefoot. Specific requirements are noted when you register.

How do I register for classes or workshops?

You can register by calling us at (555) 234-8901, emailing hello@studioaura.com, or visiting us in person at the studio. Walk-ins are welcome for regular classes when space is available, but workshops often fill quickly and we recommend booking in advance.

What is your cancellation policy?

Regular classes can be cancelled up to 4 hours before the start time for a full credit. Workshop cancellations require 48 hours notice for a refund. Late cancellations may be offered as credit toward future sessions at our discretion.

Do you offer private lessons?

Yes. Private and semi-private lessons are available in all dance forms by appointment. These sessions are tailored entirely to your goals, pace, and interests. Contact us to discuss scheduling and rates.

Is parking available?

Free street parking is available along Movement Lane and adjacent streets. There is also a public parking garage one block east of the studio on Vine Street with affordable hourly rates. We are also accessible by bus routes 12 and 37.
