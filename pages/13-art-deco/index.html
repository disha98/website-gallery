The Studio

Founded in the spirit of the golden age of dance, Studio Aura is a sanctuary for movers, dreamers, and creators. Our storied halls echo with the rhythm of a hundred styles, from the precision of classical ballet to the fire of salsa and the pulse of hip-hop. Step through our doors and discover the dancer within.

Every class is an experience. Every instructor, an artist. We believe that dance is not merely exercise—it is expression, liberation, and the most elegant language the body can speak.

Dance Forms & Fitness

Nine disciplines, one passion. Choose your rhythm.

Ballet

Classical grace and timeless technique, from barre to center.

Learn More
Our ballet program spans beginner to advanced levels, emphasizing proper alignment, musicality, and artistry. Classes include barre work, center combinations, and across-the-floor sequences rooted in Vaganova and Cecchetti traditions.

Contemporary

Fluid movement, emotional depth, and boundless creative expression.

Learn More
Blending elements of modern, jazz, and ballet, our contemporary classes explore improvisation, floorwork, and partnering. Emphasis on breath, weight, and the connection between movement and emotion.

Hip-Hop

High-energy choreography rooted in street dance culture.

Learn More
From popping and locking to new-style choreography, our hip-hop classes bring raw energy and rhythm to the floor. All levels welcome. Expect to sweat, groove, and leave with a new favorite combo.

Jazz

Sharp technique, theatrical flair, and irresistible showmanship.

Learn More
Our jazz curriculum covers Broadway, contemporary jazz, and funk styles. Classes build strength, flexibility, and performance quality through dynamic warm-ups and stylized choreography.

Salsa

Passionate Latin rhythms, partner work, and infectious energy.

Learn More
Learn the fundamentals of salsa timing, footwork, and lead-follow technique. Classes cover On1 and On2 styles, with social dancing practice and solo shine sequences.

Bollywood

Vibrant storytelling through expressive Indian cinema dance.

Learn More
A joyful fusion of classical Indian forms and modern choreography set to Bollywood film soundtracks. Classes focus on expression, hand gestures, and high-energy group routines.

Zumba

Dance-fueled cardio that feels like a celebration, not a workout.

Learn More
Our certified Zumba instructors lead classes blending Latin, international, and pop music into easy-to-follow choreography. A full-body workout disguised as the best dance party of your week.

Yoga

Mindful movement, deep stretching, and inner balance.

Learn More
Complement your dance training with vinyasa flow, restorative, and dancer-specific yoga classes. Improve flexibility, prevent injury, and cultivate the mental focus every performer needs.

Gymnastics

Acrobatic strength, tumbling, and fearless physicality.

Learn More
Build core strength, spatial awareness, and acrobatic skill through progressive gymnastics training. Classes cover tumbling, handstands, aerials, and dance-acro fusion techniques.

Questions & Answers

Do I need prior dance experience to join?
Not at all. Many of our classes are designed for complete beginners. Each discipline offers multiple levels so you can start where you are comfortable and progress at your own pace.
What should I wear to class?
Comfortable, form-fitting clothing that allows a full range of motion. Ballet classes require ballet shoes and a leotard. Hip-hop and jazz classes are best in sneakers. Yoga classes are practiced barefoot on a mat (provided).
How do I sign up for a class or workshop?
Walk-ins are welcome for regular weekly classes on a space-available basis. For workshops, we recommend registering in advance by phone or email as spots fill quickly.
Do you offer private lessons?
Yes. Private and semi-private lessons are available in all disciplines. Contact us to discuss scheduling and rates. Private lessons are perfect for wedding choreography, audition prep, or focused skill development.
What are your membership options?
We offer drop-in rates, 10-class punch cards, monthly unlimited memberships, and annual plans. Student and family discounts are available. Contact us for current pricing.
Is there parking available?
Yes. Free street parking is available on Movement Lane. A public parking garage is located one block east on Arts Boulevard. We are also accessible by bus and metro.
