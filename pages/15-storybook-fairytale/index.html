Chapter One

How They Met

♦ • ♦

A chance encounter

Summer 2019

It began, as all the best stories do, quite by accident. On a warm July morning, Priya walked into a small coffee shop on Elm Street, looking for nothing more than a good latte and a quiet corner to read. James was already there, scribbling in a notebook, his coffee growing cold beside him. Their eyes met when they both reached for the last almond croissant. He offered it to her. She split it in two. And just like that, a love story began.

Chapter Two

The Courtship

♦ • ♦

Where love bloomed

Fall 2019

Their first proper date was at the botanical garden, where the autumn leaves were turning gold and crimson. They wandered the winding paths for hours, talking about everything and nothing at all. James knew the Latin names of flowers; Priya knew which ones made the best tea. By the time they reached the garden gate, the sun was setting, and neither wanted to say goodbye. They made plans for the very next day.

Chapter Three

Building a Home Together

♦ • ♦

Their story unfolds

2021

Two years of shared mornings, evening walks, and long conversations later, they decided it was time to find a place that was truly theirs. They moved into a little apartment with tall windows and a kitchen just big enough for two. Priya filled it with books and plants. James hung fairy lights in every room. It wasn’t grand, but it was home—because they were together.

Chapter Four

The Question

♦ • ♦

A promise made in Paris

March 2025

Paris in spring is made for lovers—or so James had always believed. On a trip they’d dreamed about for years, he led Priya to a quiet bridge over the Seine at golden hour. The city hummed softly around them. He knelt, the little velvet box trembling in his hand, and asked the only question that mattered. Through joyful tears, she said yes. And the next chapter of their story was written in that single, perfect word.
