philosophy

Movement is the body's oldest language. At Studio Aura, we believe that dance is not performance alone — it is a practice of presence, a conversation between breath and form, a return to the self.

We hold space for stillness as much as motion. Every class begins in quiet, every session ends in gratitude. Here, the journey inward is as valued as the expression outward.

offerings

Ballet

Classical technique rooted in centuries of tradition. Our ballet program emphasizes alignment, grace, and the meditative quality of barre work. Classes range from foundational to advanced pointe.

Beginner through Advanced · All ages

Contemporary

Fluid, expressive movement that draws from modern and post-modern dance traditions. Emphasis on floor work, improvisation, and connecting emotion to physicality.

Intermediate & Advanced · Ages 14+

Hip-Hop

High-energy choreography grounded in street dance culture. Our instructors bring authenticity and creativity, covering styles from breaking to commercial hip-hop.

All levels · Ages 10+

Jazz

Dynamic and rhythmic, our jazz classes build strength, flexibility, and showmanship. Broadway, lyrical jazz, and funk styles explored across sessions.

All levels · Ages 12+

Salsa

Partner and solo salsa with roots in Afro-Cuban tradition. Learn timing, footwork, and the art of leading and following in a warm, social atmosphere.

All levels · No partner required

Bollywood

Vibrant, joyful choreography inspired by Indian cinema. A full-body workout that blends classical Indian gestures with contemporary Bollywood energy.

All levels · All ages welcome

Zumba

Dance-based cardio set to Latin and world rhythms. Less about precision, more about joy — a celebratory approach to fitness through movement.

No experience needed · All ages

Yoga

Vinyasa and restorative yoga sessions designed to complement a dancer's practice. Focus on breath, alignment, flexibility, and cultivating inner stillness.

All levels · Mats provided

Gymnastics

Foundational tumbling and acrobatic skills for dancers and movement enthusiasts. Builds core strength, spatial awareness, and confidence in aerial movement.

Beginner & Intermediate · Ages 6+

questions

Do I need prior dance experience to join?

Not at all. Many of our classes welcome absolute beginners. We recommend starting with any class marked "All levels" or "Beginner" and progressing at your own pace. Our instructors create a supportive, non-judgmental environment for everyone.

What should I wear to class?

Comfortable clothing that allows full range of motion. For ballet, fitted attire and ballet slippers are preferred. For hip-hop and jazz, supportive sneakers work well. Yoga and contemporary classes are typically practiced barefoot.

How does pricing work?

We offer drop-in rates, class packs of 5 or 10, and monthly unlimited memberships. Your first class is always complimentary. Workshop pricing is separate and varies by event. Please contact us for current rates.

Can I try a class before committing?

Yes. Your first class at Studio Aura is free — no commitment, no pressure. Simply arrive ten minutes early, and we will take care of the rest.

Do you offer private lessons?

We do. Private and semi-private sessions are available with all of our instructors, subject to scheduling. These are ideal for wedding choreography, performance preparation, or focused skill development.

Is there parking available?

Street parking is available along Movement Lane and adjacent streets. There is also a public parking structure one block east on Third Avenue. We encourage walking, cycling, or public transit when possible.

What is the cancellation policy?

We ask for at least four hours' notice for class cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows will be deducted from your class pack. Monthly members may cancel their membership with 30 days' written notice.
