A Chronicle of Their Courtship

From a chance meeting over coffee to a Parisian proposal — the full account

It was in the summer of 2019 that fate conspired to bring together two souls at an unassuming coffee establishment in the city. Miss Priya, arriving for her customary afternoon refreshment, found herself at the counter beside one Mr. James, who had the misfortune of upending his freshly prepared latte upon the countertop. In the small chaos that followed, introductions were made, napkins were shared, and a conversation began that neither party wished to conclude.

By the arrival of autumn that same year, the pair had arranged their first proper outing — a leisurely promenade through the Botanical Garden. Amid the changing leaves and the last warmth of the season, they discovered a shared love of conversation, long walks, and, it must be said, a mutual inability to resist dessert. Witnesses report they were last seen sharing a slice of pie at the garden cafe, laughing as though they had known each other all their lives.

The year 2021 marked a momentous chapter in their domestic history. Having spent the better part of two years in each other's constant company, the couple resolved to establish a shared household. The endeavor involved no small amount of negotiation regarding shelf space, the appropriate temperature for the thermostat, and whether one truly needs that many throw pillows. (The answer, this paper is informed, is yes.)

The most extraordinary dispatch in this chronicle arrived in March of 2025, from the City of Light itself. On a trip to Paris, France, Mr. James, having secreted a ring in his coat pocket for the better part of three anxious days, finally summoned the courage to kneel before Miss Priya at the foot of the Eiffel Tower. Through tears of unmistakable joy, she accepted. Passersby reportedly applauded. A nearby accordion player struck up a waltz.

And so, on the seventeenth of October in the year 2026, these two remarkable individuals shall pledge their lives to one another in the presence of their dearest family and friends. The Gazette is most honoured to report this news and extends its warmest felicitations to the couple. All are requested to save the date and prepare for an evening of merriment, fine dining, and enthusiastic dancing.
