About Us

Where Passion Meets Movement

Studio Aura is a premium dance and fitness studio in the heart of the Arts District. We believe movement is an art form, a therapy, and a way of life.

Our Space

3,500 sq ft of sprung hardwood floors, floor-to-ceiling mirrors, state-of-the-art sound, and atmospheric lighting designed for every mood — from meditative yoga flows to high-energy hip-hop sessions.

What We Teach

Dance fitness that doesn't feel like a workout. Latin-inspired cardio with easy-to-follow moves. Burn calories, build stamina, leave smiling.

Yoga

Restore, stretch, strengthen. Vinyasa flow and restorative sessions designed for dancers and non-dancers alike. Breathwork, alignment, and mindful movement.

All Levels 60 min Instructor: Lena Park
Gymnastics

Acrobatic foundations for dancers and fitness enthusiasts. Handstands, cartwheels, backbends, and tumbling progressions in a safe, guided environment.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need dance experience to join?
Not at all. Many of our classes are designed for complete beginners. Each class listing shows the recommended level. Our instructors are skilled at adapting to mixed-level groups, so you will always feel welcome.
What should I wear?
Comfortable clothing you can move freely in. For ballet, fitted attire and ballet slippers are recommended. For hip-hop and Zumba, sneakers work great. Yoga classes are barefoot. We have a small retail area if you need dance shoes.
How much do classes cost?
Drop-in classes are $22 each. We offer class packs (10 for $180, 20 for $320) and unlimited monthly memberships starting at $149/month. Your first class is always free.
Is there parking available?
Yes. We have a small lot behind the building with 15 spaces (first come, first served). There is also street parking and a public garage one block east on 3rd Street. Bike racks are available at our entrance.
Can I book the studio for private events?
Absolutely. Studio Aura is available for private lessons, rehearsals, photo/video shoots, and special events. Contact us for availability and rates. We offer discounted rates for nonprofit and community organizations.
Do you offer kids or teen classes?
Yes. We have dedicated youth programs for ages 6-12 and teen classes for ages 13-17 in ballet, hip-hop, and gymnastics. Check our schedule or contact us for the current youth timetable.
What is your cancellation policy?
Classes can be cancelled up to 4 hours before the start time at no charge. Late cancellations and no-shows will forfeit the class credit. Workshop cancellations require 48 hours notice for a full refund.
